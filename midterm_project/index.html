About Us

A world traveler looking to explore the wonders of the world. I went to Egypt with my family in 2024 in the late Summer. We've been to Greece, Italy, Costa Rica, Peru, Kenya and more! During our time at Egypt we got to learn the rich history that the land holds as well as sightsee countless famous landmarks ranging from the towering Pyramids to the beautiful tombs of kings and queens. I won't forget the camel ride or the the Nile River felucca tours. What's a felucca? Read more to find out and plan your next trip!
