East Bank

Luxor Temple

Luxor Temple was built in 1400 BCE. Unlike other temples, it is dedicated to the rejuvination of kingship. It is speculted that many kings were crownded here.

Luxor Museum

Luxor Museum is a small museum but it houses many artefacts and statues that it prides itself on.

Karnak Temple

Karnak Temple, unlike other temples, was built over a long period of time. It's construction started in the Middle Kingdom and continued into the Ptolemaic times. Thirty Pharaohs contributed to its buildigs.
