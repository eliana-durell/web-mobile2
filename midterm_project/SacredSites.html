Sacred Sites

The Church Of St. George

This is a Greek Orthodox church and it dates back to the 10th century.

The Hanging Church

The Hanging Church or Saint Virgin Mary's Coptic Orthodox Church is one of the oldest churches in Egypt which dates to the third century

Saints Sergius and Bacchus Church

This church is where many patriarchs of the Coptic Church were elected. It is as old as the Hanging Church and it is unknown which was built first.

Muhammad Ali Mosque

This mosque was built for Muhammad Ali Pasha between 1832 and 1857. It has an Ottoman and European style.
